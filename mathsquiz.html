2. If Sally has 5 pencils and she gives 2 to her friend, how many pencils does Sally have left?

3. What is 5 x 3?

5. If a bookshelf has 5 shelves and each shelf can hold 3 books, how many books can the bookshelf hold in total?

6. What is the perimeter of a rectangle with a length of 6 cm and a width of 4 cm?

7. If a bakery sells 250 loaves of bread per day and each loaf costs $2, how much money does the bakery make in a day?

8. f a bicycle travels 25 km in 5 hours, how many km does it travel per hour?

9. What is the next number in the pattern: 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, _?

10. If a book costs $15 and you have a $20 bill, how much change will you get?
